About Me

Hi there, I'm Shola, and I am a junior web developer who is passionate about creating functional and visually appealing websites. I may not have a traditional computer science background, but I have a lot of hard work experience that has helped me develop my skills as a developer.

I have worked in various industries, from retail to customer service, and through these experiences, I have honed my problem-solving and communication skills. I discovered my passion for web development while working on a personal projects. I realized how much I enjoyed creating something from scratch and bringing it to life on the web. Since then, I have been learning everything I can about web development and building my skills. I am a detail-oriented person who takes pride in my work and strives to deliver high-quality results. I am also a team player and enjoy working collaboratively with others to achieve project goals. I am excited to continue my journey as a web developer and to create innovative solutions that make a difference in people's lives.
